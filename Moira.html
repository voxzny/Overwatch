Moira

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Bio-Nade: Heals allies in its area while damaging and reducing healing to enemies.
  • Fade: Moira teleports a short distance, making her briefly invulnerable.
  • Necrotic Orb: Deals damage to enemies or heals allies in its path.

Ultimate Ability

Coalescence: Moira channels a powerful beam of energy that heals allies and damages enemies.
