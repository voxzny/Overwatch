Symmetra

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Photon Projector: Fires a short-range beam that intensifies over time and deals significant damage.
  • Sentry Turret: Deploys turrets that slow and damage enemies.
  • Teleporter: Creates a teleportation link between two points.

Ultimate Ability

Photon Barrier: Creates a large barrier that blocks incoming damage across the map.
