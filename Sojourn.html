Sojourn

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Railgun: Fires rapid shots that charge her railgun for a powerful secondary shot.
  • Power Slide: Slides quickly to reposition, with the option to jump at the end.
  • Disruptor Shot: Launches a shot that slows and damages enemies within its area of effect.

Ultimate Ability

Overclock: Auto-charges her railgun shots for a period, allowing for continuous high-powered shots.
