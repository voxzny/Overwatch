Pharah

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Rocket Launcher: Fires powerful rockets that deal splash damage to enemies.
  • Jump Jet: Pharah can fly for a short period, allowing for better positioning and mobility.
  • Concussive Blast: Pharah shoots a blast that pushes enemies and herself away, useful for crowd control.

Ultimate Ability

Barrage: Pharah launches a massive salvo of rockets at enemies, dealing tremendous area damage.
