Doomfist

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Rocket Punch: Charges forward, punching enemies and knocking them back.
  • Seismic Slam: Leaps to the ground, damaging and stunning nearby enemies.
  • Power Block: Absorbs incoming damage, enhancing the power of the next Rocket Punch.

Ultimate Ability

Meteor Strike: Launches into the air and crashes down at a targeted location, dealing massive damage.
