Roadhog

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Scrap Gun: A shotgun that deals both short and medium-range damage.
  • Hook: Pulls enemies toward Roadhog, stunning them briefly.
  • Take a Breather: Heals himself over a short duration, allowing for self-sustain.

Ultimate Ability

Whole Hog: Unleashes a barrage of high-damage shots from his Scrap Gun.
