Lúcio

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Sonic Amplifier: Fires a burst of sound in front of Lúcio, damaging and knocking back enemies.
  • Crossfade: Switches between two songs that either heal allies or speed them up.
  • Amp it Up: Amplifies the effect of his Crossfade, either healing or speeding up his allies even more.

Ultimate Ability

Sound Barrier: Provides a large amount of temporary shields to all nearby allies.
