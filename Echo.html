Echo

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Tri-Shot: Fires a spread of three projectiles in rapid succession.
  • Flight: Echo flies around the map for repositioning or evading attacks.
  • Focusing Beam: Fires a concentrated beam that deals increased damage to enemies under half health.

Ultimate Ability

Duplicate: Echo copies the abilities of an enemy hero, gaining access to their ultimate ability for a short time.
