Sigma

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Hyperspheres: Fires a projectile that bounces and damages enemies in its path.
  • Kinetic Grasp: Absorbs incoming projectiles and converts them into shields.
  • Accretion: Throws a mass of debris that stuns enemies.

Ultimate Ability

Gravitic Flux: Manipulates gravity to lift enemies and slam them back down, dealing significant damage.
