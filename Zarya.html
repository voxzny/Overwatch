Zarya

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Particle Cannon: A weapon that fires beams of energy, dealing more damage the more damage Zarya absorbs.
  • Barrier: Places a shield on herself or an ally to absorb damage and convert it into energy.
  • Graviton Surge: Creates a gravity well that pulls in enemies and holds them in place.

Ultimate Ability

Graviton Surge: Draws enemies into a singularity and deals massive area-of-effect damage.
