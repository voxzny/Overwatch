Sombra

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Machine Pistol: A rapid-fire weapon that deals significant damage at close range.
  • Hack: Sombra can disable enemies' abilities and health packs for a short time.
  • Thermoptic Camo: Sombra becomes invisible and faster for a short period.

Ultimate Ability

EMP: Sombra releases an EMP blast, disabling all enemy abilities and removing shields in its radius.
