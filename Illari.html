Illari

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Solar Rifle: Fires energy that can heal allies or deal damage to enemies.
  • Healing Pylon: Deploys a pylon that heals allies in its radius.
  • Solar Flare: A burst that briefly blinds and damages enemies.

Ultimate Ability

Supernova: Creates a powerful solar explosion that heals allies and damages enemies.
