Reaper

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Hellfire Shotguns: Two shotguns that deal damage at close range, with a wide spread.
  • Wraith Form: Reaper becomes invulnerable and moves faster while phasing through enemies and obstacles.
  • The Reaping: Reaper heals for a portion of damage dealt to enemies, making him resilient.

Ultimate Ability

Death Blossom: Reaper spins and unleashes a deadly barrage of shots around him, dealing massive damage in a wide area.
