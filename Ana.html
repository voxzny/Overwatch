Ana

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Biotic Rifle: A sniper rifle that heals allies or deals damage to enemies.
  • Sleep Dart: Shoots a dart that puts enemies to sleep temporarily.
  • Biotic Grenade: Throws a grenade that heals allies and amplifies damage dealt to enemies in its area.

Ultimate Ability

Nano Boost: Boosts an ally's damage and reduces incoming damage for a short time.
