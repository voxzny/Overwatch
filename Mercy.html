Mercy

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Caduceus Staff: Heals an ally or boosts their damage with the same staff.
  • Caduceus Blaster: A small semi-automatic weapon that Mercy uses for self-defense.
  • Guardian Angel: Flies toward an ally to heal them or to escape danger.

Ultimate Ability

Resurrect: Revives a dead ally, restoring them to full health.
