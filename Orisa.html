Orisa

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Augmented Fusion Driver: A projectile weapon that fires continuously.
  • Fortify: Reduces damage taken and makes Orisa immune to critical hits.
  • Protective Barrier: Deploys a stationary barrier to block enemy fire.

Ultimate Ability

Supercharger: Deploys a device that boosts damage for allies within its radius.
