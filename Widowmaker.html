Widowmaker

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Widow's Kiss: A sniper rifle that can also fire in a close-range, fully-automatic mode.
  • Grappling Hook: Allows Widowmaker to quickly reach high ground.
  • Venom Mine: A proximity mine that releases poison gas when triggered by enemies.

Ultimate Ability

Infra-Sight: Allows Widowmaker and her allies to see enemies through walls for a limited time.
