Lifeweaver

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Healing Blossom: Releases a flower that heals allies upon contact.
  • Petal Platform: Creates a platform that lifts allies or enemies into the air.
  • Rejuvenating Dash: Dashes in a direction while healing himself.

Ultimate Ability

Tree of Life: Deploys a large tree that provides healing over time to allies in its vicinity.
