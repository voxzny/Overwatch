Genji

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Shuriken: Throws three shurikens in quick succession, dealing damage in a spread.
  • Deflect: Reflects incoming projectiles back at enemies for a short duration.
  • Swift Strike: Dashes through enemies, damaging them and resetting the cooldown if an enemy is hit.

Ultimate Ability

Dragonblade: Genji unsheathes his sword and performs a flurry of slashes, dealing massive damage to enemies in front of him.
