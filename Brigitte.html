Brigitte

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Rocket Flail: A melee weapon that deals damage in an arc and knocks enemies back.
  • Repair Pack: Heals an ally and provides them with bonus armor.
  • Whip Shot: Throws her flail, stunning and knocking back enemies in a line.

Ultimate Ability

Rally: Brigitte charges forward, healing nearby allies and providing them with armor.
