Winston

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Tesla Cannon: A short-range weapon that deals continuous damage to enemies in front of him.
  • Jump Pack: Allows Winston to jump to a location, ideal for repositioning or escaping.
  • Barrier Projector: Deploys a shield that protects Winston and his allies.

Ultimate Ability

Primal Rage: Winston slams the ground, knocking enemies back and into the air.
