Junkrat

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Frag Launcher: Launches grenades that explode after a delay, dealing area damage.
  • Concussion Mine: A mine that deals damage and knocks enemies back when triggered.
  • Steel Trap: Deploys a trap that ensnares enemies who walk into it, immobilizing them.

Ultimate Ability

RIP-Tire: Junkrat deploys a tire that he can control, rolling it into enemies for a massive explosion.
