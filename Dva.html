D.Va

Role: Tank

Abilities:

  • Fusion Cannons: Rapid-fire weapons with no ammo cost.
  • Defense Matrix: Absorbs incoming projectiles in front of her.
  • Boosters: D.Va flies around using boosters to reposition.
  • Micro Missiles: Launches missiles in a burst for extra damage.

Ultimate Ability

Self-Destruct: D.Va ejects from her mech and the mech explodes, dealing massive area damage.
