Cassidy

Role: Damage

Abilities:

  • Peacekeeper: A revolver with a high rate of fire and high damage output.
  • Combat Roll: Cassidy rolls to reload his revolver and gain bonus ammo.
  • Flashbang: Throws a grenade that stuns enemies within its blast radius.

Ultimate Ability

Deadeye: Locks onto enemies and then fires a powerful shot at each locked target.
