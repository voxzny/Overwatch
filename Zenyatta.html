Zenyatta

Role: Support

Abilities:

  • Orb of Destruction: Fires orbs individually or in a rapid succession, dealing damage to enemies.
  • Orb of Harmony: Attaches to an ally and heals them over time as long as Zenyatta maintains line of sight.
  • Orb of Discord: Attaches to an enemy, increasing the damage they take as long as Zenyatta maintains line of sight.

Ultimate Ability

Transcendence: Zenyatta becomes invulnerable and rapidly heals nearby allies for a short duration.
